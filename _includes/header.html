I am a Ph.D. student in the Information Systems department at the University of Maryland Baltimore County (UMBC), advised by Dr. Sanjay Purushotham. I am working as a graduate research assistant at Dr. Sanjay's lab. My research focus is on the application of machine learning and statistical learning to healthcare. My research interests include statistics, machine learning, deep learning, survival analysis, causal inference, explainable AI, quantifying uncertainty in machine learning models. I completed my B.Sc. and M.Sc. in Statistics from the University of Dhaka, Bangladesh. Before joining UMBC, I was an assistant director in the statistics department of the central bank of Bangladesh.
